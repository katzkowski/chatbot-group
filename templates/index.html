What is Elephanture?

Elephanture is a modern chatbot adventure game with a story in combination with AI. It also has many features of RPGs to make an immersive game experience.

You can create an account to save your game state and start your discovery.

Modern AI technology

We are using the GPT-2 with a self-trained model for text generation in the game. This is used for talking to the main NPC and to make object and environment descriptions more interesting.

Every time you will play a part of the game again, it will feel different and consistantly give you new impressions of the setting.

Story

In the text adventure ‘Elephanture’ you are playing as a student of the University of Bremen. To achieve your goal of getting a bachelor degree, you need to overcome a lot of obstacles and difficult riddles. On your mission, you explore various parts of Bremen and get to know the city and it's history.

Will you be able to uncover the myth-enshrouded Elephant Monument and it's secret about the blood diamonds?

Team - University of Bremen

We are students from the University of Bremen and this is our Bachelor Project. Our research topic is “Chatbots” and in this project we study how to improve the traditional text adventure game by including state of the art technology like the GPT-2 model.
